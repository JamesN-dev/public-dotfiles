##My Neovim Config Cheatsheet

:w			Save
:q			Quit
:wq			Save & Quit
:q!			Force Quit

yap			Yank a paragraph
p	 		Paste



##Navigation
--Split Screen
ctrl+w+v 		Right Split
ctrl+w+s		Bottom Split 
--Window focus
<Ctrl-w><Ctrl-h>	move focus to left window
<Ctrl-w><Ctrl-l>	move focus to right window
<Ctrl-w><Ctrl-j>	move focus to lower window
<Ctrl-w><Ctrl-k>	move focus to upper window
